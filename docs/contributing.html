üìã Research Guidelines

Scientific Rigor: All contributions should maintain high scientific standards. Speculation is welcome, but clearly distinguish between established facts, working hypotheses, and speculative ideas.

Open Collaboration: Research should be open and accessible. Share methods, data, and code whenever possible to enable reproducibility and collaboration.

Ethical Considerations: Consider the philosophical and ethical implications of consciousness research. Treat all perspectives with respect while maintaining critical thinking.

Interdisciplinary Approach: Welcome insights from physics, neuroscience, philosophy, computer science, and even contemplative traditions. The nature of consciousness requires diverse perspectives.
